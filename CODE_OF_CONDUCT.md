# Code of Conduct

This project is maintained by a small team. We want interactions here to be productive and professional.

## Rules

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions, issues, and pull requests.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or spam.
- Keep conversations on topic and relevant to the project.
- Do not share others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

If someone violates these rules, maintainers may warn, mute, or ban them at their discretion.

## Reporting

Report issues to `sgzrov@gmail.com`. Reports are handled privately.
